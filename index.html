FAQ & SUPPORT

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about our internet services, installation, and support

Our fiber plans range from 50 Mbps to 1 Gbps depending on your package. Residential plans offer up to 500 Mbps, while business plans can go up to 1 Gbps symmetrical (same upload and download speeds). All our connections are fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) for maximum reliability.
No long-term contracts! We offer month-to-month subscriptions. You can cancel anytime with a 30-day notice. For business plans, we offer flexible terms ranging from month-to-month to 12-month agreements with volume discounts.
Standard installation takes 24-48 hours after you sign up. Our certified technicians handle everything from fiber cabling to router configuration. For complex installations or apartments, it may take 3-5 days. We offer same-day installation for select areas (subject to availability).
We currently cover most Nairobi areas including Westlands, Karen, Lavington, Runda, Kileleshwa, Kilimani, Hurlingham, Parklands, and Upper Hill. We're expanding to Kiambu Road, Ongata Rongai, and Syokimau soon. Enter your area in our coverage checker to confirm availability.
Yes! Static IP addresses are available for business plans starting from our Pro package upward. For residential customers, you can add a static IP for an additional KSh 500/month. Perfect for hosting servers, VPNs, or remote access.
Our 24/7 support team is always available. Call our hotline, WhatsApp us, or email. Technical issues are typically resolved within 2-4 hours. We also have a real-time network status page where you can check for outages. For business customers, we offer 4-hour response SLAs.
